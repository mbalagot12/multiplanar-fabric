Floor busway required (Starline / Legrand) — standard PDU-per-rack cannot handle 135 kW density.
MRC wiring rule
CX-8 NIC n of every tray → leaf of plane n. Each leaf has 9× 800G downlinks (one per server) and uplinks to its own dedicated 7060XE7 spine. No cross-plane links. MRC 32-bit EV striped across UDP src port + IPv6 flow label, encoded in SRv6 uN SID. 128–256 EVs generated per QP at startup, equally split across planes. Fabric switches are stateless forwarders — zero ECMP, no dynamic routing.
