    NVIDIA GB300 NVL72 — Liquid-Cooled Data Center Rack

    48U MGX rack  ·  18 compute trays  ·  9 NVLink switch trays  ·  CDU cooling loop  ·  72× B300 GPUs  ·  135 kW TDP  ·  1.1 ExaFLOPS FP4

    Compute trays
    B300 GPU chip (288 GB HBM3e · 15 PFLOPS FP4 · 1,400 W)
    NVLink switch tray (5th-gen · 1.8 TB/s per GPU)
    Cold plate (GPU · CPU · NIC liquid-cooled)
    Grace ARM CPU (×2 per tray)
    Networking
    CX-8 SuperNIC (800 Gb/s per port · 1:1 GPU:NIC)
    BlueField-3 DPU (400 Gb/s)
    Arista 7060XE7 leaf switch per plane
    Cooling — hybrid DLC
    Cold supply line (facility water 18–22°C)
    Hot return line (exits at 40–45°C)
    CDU — in-rack (250 kW) or in-row (1.8 MW)
    GPUs, CPUs, NVSwitch & CX-8 NICs: liquid cold plates. OSFP modules, storage, PDB: air-cooled (hybrid architecture).
    Power
    PSU shelf (33 kW × 8 = 264 kW headroom)
    DC bus bar (48V to all trays)
    Floor busway required (Starline / Legrand) — standard PDU-per-rack cannot handle 135 kW density.
    MRC wiring rule
    CX-8 NIC n of every tray → leaf of plane n. Each leaf has 9× 800G downlinks (one per server) and uplinks to its own dedicated 7060XE7 spine. No cross-plane links. MRC 32-bit EV striped across UDP src port + IPv6 flow label, encoded in SRv6 uN SID. 128–256 EVs generated per QP at startup, equally split across planes. Fabric switches are stateless forwarders — zero ECMP, no dynamic routing.
